Content modelling is an essential tool for designing content-rich sites and cross-channel publishing. A content model defines content types and their relationships to one another. It allows you to design structured, reusable content that can go from desktop to mobile and beyond. In other words, content modelling enables to you get to ‘true’ adaptive and responsive designs.

In this hands-on session, you will learn about how to create your own content model and use it to inform your designs, user journeys, and CMS requirements.

The topics include:

* what content modelling is 
* why content modelling is important 
* how content models are used (case studies) 
* how to create a content model that supports your business needs
