Front-end pattern libraries are hot news in the UX community these days. Anna Debenham wrote a book on it and A List Apart just open-sourced its pattern library for all to see. 

At PebbleRoad we've been giving clients pattern libraries as a deliverable. We even have our own library called Tapestry that we use to develop prototypes. 

In this session, Vinay Malankad will give a behind the scenes look at how we use pattern libraries and show you how you can build one of your own!
