Title: Vasu Kolla

----

Summary: Vasu specialises in information architecture and usability for large websites. He has led major projects for clients such as Nokia, SingTel and Asian Development Bank. Vasu also contributes to the local design community through his involvement with IxDA and by teaching design courses. He has a Master’s in Information Systems from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.
