Title: Prakriti Parijat

----

Summary: Prakriti is a senior user experience designer at PebbleRoad. She has over 7 years of experience in user research and design. She has led a variety of UX design projects for clients such as GSK, StarHub, Nokia, Bosch, DBS, and Citibank. Prakriti has extensive experience in training that includes teaching postgraduate students. Prakriti has a PhD from Virginia Tech, USA in the field of Human Factors and Ergonomics.
