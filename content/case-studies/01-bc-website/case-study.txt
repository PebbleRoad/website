Title: British Council - website

----

Summary: We helped the British Council Singapore double the number of visitors to their website.

----

Category: User Research, Content Strategy, Information Architecture

----

Client: British Council Singapore

----

Industry: Non-profit

----

Keychallenge: The challenge was to increase engagement and registrations and make the Council’s offerings sharper.

----

Concept: We did a deep dive to understand the British Council’s business model and the unique propositions that it brought to the table. We also researched the target audience to find out their key needs and desires. In addition to making the information architecture and the content sharper, we also helped design a series of stories that showcased the language teaching expertise of the British Council.

----

Outcome: The number of visitors to the website doubled after the redesign.
