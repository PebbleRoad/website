## What it is
A free app to display data in Google Fusion Tables in HTML table format.

## Why use it
This may be part of a workflow to take [data tables stuck in PDF files to the web](http://www.pebbleroad.com/perspectives/move-data-tables-from-pdf-to-web). The first part is to move the data tables in PDF format to Google Fusion Tables. Then, this app can help in taking the the table in Google Fusion Tables to the web.
